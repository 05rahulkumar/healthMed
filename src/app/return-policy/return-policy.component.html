Return Policy
Return Conditions

Products can be returned within 30 days of delivery. The product must be in its original condition, unused, and with all tags and packaging intact.

How to Return
  1. Log in to your account and go to the "My Orders" section.
  2. Select the order you want to return and click on "Return Item".
  3. Follow the instructions to complete the return request.
Refund Policy

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you about the status of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.
